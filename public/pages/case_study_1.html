CASE STUDY

Introduction and Overview:

  • Provide a brief introduction to the ideation portfolio.
  • Include an overview of the problem or challenge you're addressing.

Problem Statement:

  • Clearly articulate the problem or challenge that your ideation addresses
  • Define the scope and boundaries of the problem.

User Personas:

  • Identify and describe the target audience or users for your solution.
  • Include relevant details about their needs, preferences, and pain points.

Research Insights:

  • Summarize key findings from any research conducted, such as user interviews, surveys, or market analysis
  • Highlight important data or quotes that informed your ideation process.

Inspiration Board:

  • Create a visual inspiration board with images, quotes, and examples that inspired your ideation.
  • Mind Maps:
  • Use mind maps to visually represent the interconnected ideas related to your solution.
  • Include references to existing products, designs, or concepts that align with your goals.
  • Branch out from central concepts to explore different aspects and potential solutions.

Brainstorming Session Outputs:

  • Document the results of brainstorming sessions in Miro.
  • Include images, sketches, or diagrams that capture the diverse ideas generated.

Concept Sketches or Wireframes:

  • Develop initial concept sketches or wireframes to visualize potential solutions.
  • Include annotations or explanations to clarify design choices.

Prototypes:

  • If applicable, include interactive prototypes of your ideas.
  • Use Miro's features to create clickable links or hotspot areas for user interaction.

Feedback and Iteration:

  • Present feedback received from stakeholders, team members, or potential users.
  • Show how the ideation has evolved through iterative processes.

Feasibility Assessment:

  • Discuss the feasibility of implementing your ideas, considering technical, financial, and resource constraints.
  • Identify potential challenges and propose solutions.

Final Concept and Solution:

  • Showcase the refined and finalized concept or solution.
  • Provide a clear and concise description of the proposed solution.

Next Steps and Action Plan:

  • Outline the next steps in the development process.
  • Define an action plan with milestones, tasks, and responsibilities.

Conclusion:

  • Summarize the key points and emphasize the significance of your ideation.
  • Encourage collaboration and further exploration.

Appendix:

  • Include any additional documentation, references, or supplementary materials.
